Website Details

HEYYYY! If you clicked this section, then chances are you don’t know me, or you do and want to see how I am portraying myself LOL. So here it goes… My name is Saima, I am 25 years old (although, I like to think I am still 21, like any other person this close to 30). I am originally from the UK, Birmingham to be more specific *queue all the Birmingham memes*. I moved to the UAE at the end of August 2018, so it is still something very new and fresh to me. I love travelling, and have visited 24 countries and counting. My aim this year was to visit at least 5 new countries, however I am 6 months into 2019 and I have only visited 2. Disappointing right? The purpose of this blog is to share my experience living out in the UAE, travel, experiences, views and food recommendations and just a general blog from me to you. I love sharing my experiences and advising/helping people and writing as this blog will entail. I am a strong feminist (not a misandrist before you start rolling your eyes, at yet another ‘feminist’). Fighting patriarchy comes natural; after all, I am a Sociology graduate. I would also like to share my experiences as a South Asian female. I am breaking away from a culture which favours males, promotes marriage as being your only chance of ‘freedom’ and frowns upon females having a voice. I am a huge self love enthusiast. I have battled (some days it is still battling) with anxiety, depression and my fair share life changing experiences. I am here to tell all, in hope to help you!
